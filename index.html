Are you drinking enough water?

According to the NHS, adult humain beings should drink at least 1.2 litres (six to eight glasses) of fluid every day.

How can you easily increase your water intake?

Make it a habit: Drink a glass of water when you wake up. Make it sustainable: Buy a reusable water bottle instead of buying a plastic one everytime. Make it fun: Add flavouring to your water by using herbs or fresh fruit.

Why is it important to eat well?

It can help reduce the risk of diseases such as cancer and arthritis, it helps maintain a healthy weight and it enhances your mood.
