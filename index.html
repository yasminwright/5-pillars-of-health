Water

Are you drinking enough water?

According to the NHS, adults should drink at least 1.2 litres (six to eight glasses) of fluid every day.

How can you easily increase your water intake?

  • Make it a habit: Drink a glass of water when you wake up.
  • Make it sustainable: Buy a reusable water bottle instead of buying a plastic one everytime.
  • Make it fun: Add flavouring to your water by using herbs or fresh fruit.
